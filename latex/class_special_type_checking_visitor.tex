{\footnotesize\ttfamily void Special\+Type\+Checking\+Visitor\+::register\+Before\+Visit\+Children (\begin{DoxyParamCaption}{ }\end{DoxyParamCaption})}

Remembers the number of nodes in types\+Visited\+Nodes. After the children have been visited, calling discard\+Children\+Datatypes removes the datatype of the visited children from the stack. This mechanism is required for nodes that do not require children\textquotesingle{}s type information or where the number of children is arbitrary and hence a simple pop() after visiting the children is not sufficient.
